So Why a Digital Portfolio?

A digital Portfolio Helps a creator to post out is work to the real world. This developer can now show the work he has done and the projects he made to people who can offer him job oppurtunities. It is kind of like a digital resume, for computer science. A way for people to see you expertise in web design and the projects you made. An excellent tool to brag about your work and show off to the people that you care about, specifically people who can give job oppurtunities
