So Why a Digital Portfolio?

A digital Portfolio Helps a creator to post out is work to the real world. This developer can now show the work he has done and the projects he made to people who can offer him job oppurtunities. It is kind of like a digital resume, for computer science. A way for people to see you expertise in web design and the projects you made. An excellent tool to brag about your work and show off to the people that you care about, specifically people who can give job oppurtunities. A digital portfolio also helps people to show of their soft skills in website development, which is a very crucial component of any career. A way to show off to their bosses their talent in web design. As a background in computer applications becomes more in demand every yearfor tech and non-tech companies, a digital portoflio is a stupendous way to prove your efforts in computer science, and computer applications, making anyone with a digital portfolio, a favored candidate over people without one.
